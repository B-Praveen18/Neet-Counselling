# Neet-Counselling
Normal neet counselling management implementation 

1).PERSPECTIVE 
The purpose of this project is to introduce the NEET Counselling Management System, a platform designed to simplify and digitalize the counselling and seat-allocation process for NEET candidates. The system ensures that students can register, upload documents, select colleges and courses, and receive allotment results in a transparent and user-friendly manner.
When a student logs into the system for the first time, they are required to create an account and complete their profile. After successful login, the application allows students to enter their rank,and preferences. Based on the information provided, the system displays a list of colleges and courses that match the student’s eligibility and preferences. The student can then lock their choices and wait for the allotment result.
→ Student – They can register, submit documents, choose preferred colleges/courses, and view allotment results.  

2.)OBJECTIVE
The main objective of the NEET Counselling Management System is to provide a smooth, transparent, and efficient counselling process for NEET aspirants. The system aims to reduce manual errors, provide accurate seat allocation, and help students receive timely updates regarding counselling rounds. It also ensures that the entire process—from registration to final allotment—is managed systematically and securely.

3.)SCOPE
The scope of this project is to assist NEET candidates in completing all counselling-related activities through a single platform. The system supports registration, document verification, choice filling, seat allotment, and reporting. Additionally, it reduces waiting time, eliminates the need for physical visits, and ensures that students receive correct and real-time information regarding available seats and their admissions status.
